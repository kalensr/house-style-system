# Positive Leadership Reflection Control

I was reviewing a decision that looked easy on paper. The pressure was not the choice itself; it was whether I could explain the path well enough for the team to trust it.

The question I kept coming back to was simple: what work would make the next step clear? I needed to slow down, name the evidence, and show the sequence.

That gave me the principle. Vision helps people when it becomes a roadmap they can inspect. The next step is to write the path, name the owner, and test the plan with the people who will use it.
